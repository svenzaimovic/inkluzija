Many children with disabilities are either not registered in the regular education system or are not included at all, and they certainly do not receive adequate support required for successful inclusion.

Tuzla Canton has substantial capacities to implement inclusion through human resources available at the Faculty of Special Education and Rehabilitation, Pedagogical Institute of Tuzla Canton, non-governmental organisations and future specialised institutes transformed into centres for development of inclusive practices.

DEVELOPMENT OF THE TUZLA CANTON STRATEGY INVOLVED:

It is a major investment at a minimal cost

It completely rejects the approach that sees children/students with disabilities as passive recipients of medical and/or other types of care (under the medical model)

The emphasis is on the abilities of each child/student, who can, after the society removes all existing obstacles, achieve their full potential.

The strategy has been adopted by the Government of Tuzla Sarajevo


THE FOLLOWING STEPS:

Over the next three years, the Ministry of Education and Science of Tuzla Canton, in cooperation with Save the Children and with financial support from the Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, will establish the first two centres for development of inclusive practices and initiate the process of transformation of special schools in the Tuzla Canton. This cooperation will take place as part of the project Inclusion4All and in partnership with the Knowledge Centre Profectus from Croatia and Vesta Association. In addition to these activities, the project also aims to strengthen civil society organisations to support inclusion and publicly advocate inclusion of children with disabilities and their families.
