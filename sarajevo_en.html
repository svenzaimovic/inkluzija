Development of the Canton Sarajevo Strategy involved:

The Strategy is a major investment at a minimal cost

Canton Sarajevo already has many good inclusion practices, with trained staff in primary schools, specialised institutions, University of Sarajevo, Pedagogical Institute of Canton Sarajevo and non-governmental organisations.

  • This strategy systematically structures services already in place as well as those under development
  • Provides clear direction for transition from the medical approach to children with developmental disabilities to a human rights model
  • New forms of support are introduced through establishment of centres for development of inclusive practices

The Strategy focuses on inclusion as the only option for preschool upbringing and education of students with developmental disabilities in Canton Sarajevo. The strategy defines a clear system for early detection and intervention and for the flow of information about support needs from preschool, via primary and through to secondary education institutions, in order to maintain the continuity of support throughout education.
